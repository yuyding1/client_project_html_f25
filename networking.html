Networking & Connections

How can Networking help me achieve my career goals?

Networking is key to career growth, but reaching out can feel daunting. This page is to help you debunking the mystery of networking through creating a strategic networking plan and learning outreach methods.

Building a Strategic Networking Plan

Creating a LAMP list can help job seekers identify and prioritize a list of target organizations for strategic networking before actively applying for jobs or internships.

  1. Creating a Spreadsheet: Use Excel or Google Spreadsheets to create a spreadsheet provided by UMSI.
  2. Creating 5 Columns: One to number the list (1-40), one to write the name of the organizations, one for alumni, one for motivation, and one for postings.
  3. Creating Your List of 40 Organizations: 10 Dream Organizations, 10 Alumni Organizations, 10 Organizations with Job Postings, and 10 Trending or Open to Sponsorship Organizations.
  4. Ranking Your List of 40 Organizations: Start ranking your 40 organizations based on your motivation or interest.
  5. Filling in the Posting Column: Finding out if the organizations on your list are currently hiring, using a job posting aggregator such as Indeed.
