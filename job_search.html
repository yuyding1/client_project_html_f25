Job-Searching Skill

Learn How to Search a Job

Finding the right job opportunities can be overwhelming. Don't worry! This page offers you with example frameworks and available resources as a UMSI student to guide your job search and help you take the next step with confidence.

Planning Your Job Search

  1. U-M Resources: Using CareerLink and Handshake as part of your internship search, but there are many other resources, people, events, etc. available to you as a U-M student.
  2. Company Websites: Focusing on a specific industry and/or geographic location can allow you to find new companies you weren’t aware of, as well as internship opportunities.
  3. National Job Boards: These job boards can provide a wide range of job/internship opportunities.
  4. Industry-Specific Resources: It’s helpful to look for more specific resources, such as a related student organization, professional association, listserv, LinkedIn group, etc.
  5. Networking: Year after year UMSI students rank networking as the most helpful resource that leads to their internship and job offers.
  6. Prioritized Action Steps: List two things you can accomplish within the next weeks.

  • Job & Internship Search Checklist
